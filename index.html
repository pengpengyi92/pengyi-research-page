Research Motivation

Financial research is not only an information-retrieval problem. It requires evidence, temporal context, entity relationships, assumptions, uncertainty, and human review. I want to study reliable agentic systems that preserve these constraints while helping researchers move from messy information to reproducible analysis.

Background

My background combines mathematics and complex systems training at King's College London, WorldQuant-style alpha research, AI research-infrastructure study, and front-line exposure to corporate banking and industry-chain finance.
